# Where You Lead From — The Energy You Bring

An IDEAL leadership reflection game. Across 8 situations, choose your **First Instinct** and **Second Move** to reveal the leadership energy you reach for first — Sovereign, Warrior, Lover/Connector or Jester — and the energy you may need to activate more deliberately.

**Play it here:** https://tanyl85-coder.github.io/where-you-lead-from/

- A facilitated reflection exercise — not a validated psychometric instrument.
- All responses stay in the player's browser (localStorage). Nothing is collected or transmitted.
- Facilitators: the in-app room view aggregates anonymous result codes (unlocks at 5+ participants).
